Our Product

A.I Smart Socket:
Experience unparalleled comfort and adaptability with our AI-Smart Socket, featuring dynamic auto-fit technology, advanced moisture control, real-time user feedback, and AI-powered customization—all designed to perfectly fit your lifestyle.

Product Features

1. Climate Control

Comfort in Every Climate: Our advanced control system actively manages moisture and temperature within the socket, ensuring a dry and cool environment, reducing skin irritation and discomfort.

2. Auto Fit

Perfect Fit, Every Time: The auto-fit system dynamically adjusts the socket's fit in real-time, responding to changes in limb size and shape, providing a secure and comfortable experience.

3. Smart Sensing with Haptics

Stay Informed: Integrated sensors monitor pressure, humidity, and temperature within the socket, delivering real-time feedback through haptics for maximum comfort and safety.

4. Adaptive Liner Technology

Comfort That Adapts: Our liner absorbs moisture and offers superior cushioning, conforming to the limb’s shape for a custom fit and reduced pressure points.

5. TRACE Technology

Easy Customization: Our mobile app uses image processing to create a 3D model of your socket, making customization quick and easy.

6. Live Monitoring

Real-time Dashboard: Monitor humidity, temperature, and pressure within the socket, ensuring optimal comfort and performance.

7. Flex Mod

Adaptive Support: The socket automatically adjusts its stiffness based on activity levels and environmental conditions for enhanced comfort and mobility.

8. AI Integration

Advanced AI: Our smart socket integrates AI to learn and adapt to user behavior, enhancing the overall user experience over time.

About Us

We are an early-stage startup focused on developing cutting-edge wearable technology solutions for prosthetic and orthotic devices. Currently, we are in collaboration with the I-Hub Foundation for Cobotics at IIT Delhi.

Mission
We at Biokinetic labs envision a future where individuals with disabilities and different abilities are empowered not just to restore their biomechanical functions, but also to enhance and augment their capabilities through innovative robotic solutions.
